About Ivy Alley Social House

Welcome to Ivy Alley Social House — where everyone’s invited and there’s something for every kind of fun. From state-of-the-art duckpin bowling to live music and family-friendly entertainment, we’re a place to gather, relax, and make memories.

Our full kitchen specializes in high-temp, stone-oven pizza, but we also serve a fresh selection of sandwiches, soups, and salads for every taste. Settle in with a pint from our on-site microbrewery or sip an expertly crafted cocktail from our full bar — whatever you’re craving, we’ve got you covered.

Eight Lanes of Fun

With eight duckpin lanes, there’s plenty of room for everyone — though reservations are always welcome if you want to guarantee your spot.

Duckpin bowling features shorter lanes and smaller balls, making it easy for kids to join the fun while still challenging even the most competitive adults. And yes, it's true: getting a strike is tougher than it looks. Pro tip: roll slower than you think!

Pizza Fired at 750°F

Our roaring 750°F oven turns out irresistibly crisp, thin-crust pizzas loaded with flavor. We start with handmade, cold-fermented dough, add your choice of house-made sauces, and top it with fresh mozzarella, provolone, and your favorite toppings.

Mix, match, and create your perfect pie.

Craft Beer & Creative Cocktails

At the heart of our bar is our microbrewery, where we transform grain, hops, and water into a variety of distinctive beers.

Prefer something else? We proudly feature local distilleries and wineries alongside the classics you know and love. Choose from margaritas on tap, a freshly made Old Fashioned, or a non-alcoholic cocktail inspired by our friends at FunktioN/al in Mishawaka.
