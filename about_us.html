About Ivy Alley Social House

Ivy Alley Social House is where great food, craft drinks, and unforgettable experiences come together. Whether you're here for duckpin bowling, live music, or a night out with friends, we’ve created a space where everyone feels welcome.

Enjoy stone‑oven pizza, sandwiches, soups, salads, and a full bar featuring our on‑site microbrewery. From family outings to date nights, Ivy Alley is your new go‑to spot for good times.

Eight Lanes of Fun

Our duckpin lanes offer fast‑paced fun for all ages. Smaller balls, shorter lanes, and a lively atmosphere make it perfect for families and competitive groups alike.

Reservations are welcome — especially on busy nights — but walk‑ins are always encouraged.

Pizza Fired at 750°F

Our stone oven fires at 750°F, creating crisp, thin‑crust pizzas with bold flavor. Handmade dough, house‑made sauces, and fresh toppings make every pie unforgettable.

Build your own or choose from our signature creations.

Craft Beer & Creative Cocktails

Our microbrewery produces a rotating selection of beers — from crisp lagers to bold ales. Prefer spirits? Our bar features local distilleries, classic cocktails, and creative non‑alcoholic options.

Whether you're sipping a margarita on tap or trying something new, there’s always something to enjoy.
