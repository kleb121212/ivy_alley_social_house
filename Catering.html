Catering & Private Parties

Ivy Alley Social House offers catering and private event hosting in the heart of downtown South Bend. Whether you're planning a birthday celebration, corporate gathering, team outing, or family event, our space blends great food, craft drinks, and duckpin bowling for a memorable experience.

Why Host Your Event at Ivy Alley
