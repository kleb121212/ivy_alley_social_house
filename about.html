What Is Duckpin Bowling?

Duckpin bowling uses smaller balls and shorter pins, making the game more challenging and fun.

Players get three rolls per frame, and the balls have no finger holes—perfect for all ages and skill levels.

House Rules

  • Please do not stand on the lanes
  • No lobbing or bouncing the balls
  • No trick shots
  • Only roll when the light is green
  • Act like your grandma is watching 😉
  • Have fun!

Damaged balls may result in a $110 replacement fee.
