About Ivy Alley Social House

Ivy Alley Social House is a neighborhood gathering spot in downtown South Bend, Indiana, blending duckpin bowling, craft beer, and a welcoming food menu into one cozy, social space. Whether you're here for a casual night out, a family get-together, or a private event, Ivy Alley is designed for connection, conversation, and good times.
